{# dashboard-kit — toast seed (server-rendered notification → floating toast). `toast(message, severity, flag, banner, validation)` renders ONE hidden marker element. kit.js (section 6) scans for [data-toast] on page load AND after every htmx swap, lifts each seed into a floating, auto-dismissing toast inside the #toast-host overlay, then removes the seed.
